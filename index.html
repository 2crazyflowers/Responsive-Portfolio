About Me

I am learning web development in a fullstack bootcamp through the University of Minnesota. I enjoy coding and debugging websites. I love it when I get websites to look right and work perfectly!

Previously I worked as a landscape designer and project manager for 5 years then went to bookkeeping and office management for 10 years. I got interested in web development during my previous job and found that it was fun and I wanted to learn more! Once my classes are complete I am looking forward to a career change into web development.

I am married to a great guy, have 2 children (who keep me on my toes and astonish me daily) as well as 2 cats, 1 dog and 3 aquariums (and yes, we have a very full house!). I love spending time with my family and friends, traveling, hiking, kayaking and fixing up houses.
